All Ears

Protecting forest elephants

Our Mission

We intend through this project to educate people to the threat of forest elephant poaching. This poaching is having a devastating effect on the environment of the rainforests in the Congo Basin and is accelerating global warming. We intend to effectively illustrate this to the public so that they can take action.
